Featured Story

Two years with the
Messenger. No notes.

James Kato bought the AETHER Messenger in the first week of its release. He has taken it to 14 countries, through a monsoon in Kyoto, and across a film set in the Atacama. The leather has darkened in the strap where he carries it. He has not cleaned it once.

"I stopped thinking about the bag about two weeks in. That's what I wanted — something that just disappears until you need it. It does that."
