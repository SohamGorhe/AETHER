Sizing & Fit
Our sneakers run true to size with a slightly wide fit. If you have narrow feet, we recommend sizing down half a size. The leather upper will soften and mould to your foot over the first two to three weeks of wear.
Yes, all sneaker styles are available in half sizes from EU 39.5 to EU 46.5. If your half size is sold out, email us — we may be able to source from upcoming production.
Bags are one size. Outerwear is cut to layer over knitwear — full chest measurement plus 4cm at the wrist. We recommend sizing up if you plan to layer heavily underneath.
Shipping
Yes. Standard worldwide shipping is free on all orders and takes 3–5 business days. Express shipping (1–2 days) is available for $24. Same-day delivery is available in select cities.
Every order ships in a reusable linen dust bag inside an unbleached kraft box. No plastic, no excessive tissue paper. The packaging is designed to be kept and reused.
You will receive a tracking link by email when your order ships. You can also track your order from your account page at any time.
Returns & Repairs
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn and in their original packaging. Exchanges for a different size are free of charge.
We will repair, resole, restitch, recondition, or replace hardware on any AETHER product for as long as you own it. There is no end date and no charge for the repair itself — you only pay shipping to send the item to us.
Email repairs@aether.com with your order number and a photo of the item. We will confirm the repair, send you a pre-paid shipping label, and return your item within 2–4 weeks.
Materials & Care
Clean with a slightly damp cloth. Condition with natural beeswax every 3–6 months to maintain suppleness. Avoid direct sunlight for extended periods. The leather will develop a patina unique to your use — this is not damage, it is the material working as intended.
Machine wash cold on a gentle cycle. Tumble dry low. Re-wax the shell annually using Halley Stevensons wax or a neutral beeswax. Do not dry clean.
Sneakers are made in Portugal. Bags are made in England. Outerwear is made in Scotland and Portugal. Everyday objects are made in England. All material sourcing and final quality inspection happens in-house.
